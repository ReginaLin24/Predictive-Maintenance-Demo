EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:
Seal leak detected in PUMP_009 (HydroMax-5000) during routine inspection. Medium patient impact 
classification. Service performed during scheduled maintenance window.

DIAGNOSTIC FINDINGS:
- Visual inspection revealed moisture around primary seal
- Pressure drop of 3.2 PSI observed over 24-hour period
- Chlorine level inconsistency in downstream monitoring
- Fault Code FC_006 logged: Seal Leak Detected

ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS:

chemical compatibility stress from chlorine exposure over time. Seal hardness testing indicated 
25% degradation from specification.

CORRECTIVE ACTIONS TAKEN:
1. Controlled shutdown initiated
2. Primary seal assembly removal and inspection
3. Seal housing surface preparation and cleaning
4. Installation of upgraded seal design (improved chlorine resistance)
5. Pressure testing to 150% operating pressure
6. 2-hour operational verification
